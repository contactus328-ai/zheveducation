Study in USA

The USA offers world-leading education with strong career pathways in STEM fields like Computer Science, AI/ML, Robotics, Data Science and Cybersecurity. Popular programs also include MBA, Healthcare, Music, Film, Sports Management, Fashion, Architecture and Game Design. OPT/CPT pathways allow valuable work experience after graduation.

Study in Canada

Canada is ideal for PR pathways and affordable education. Popular programs include MS in CS, Data Analytics, Nursing, Hotel Management, Culinary Arts, Healthcare, Architecture and early childhood education. Co-op programs provide strong industry exposure.

Study in UK & Ireland

The UK offers 1-year Master’s programs in Management, Engineering, Fashion, Film, Sports Science, Hospitality, Architecture and Business. Ireland is perfect for tech courses like Software Engineering, AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity and Pharmaceuticals, with a 2-year PSW visa.

Study in Australia & New Zealand

Known for PR-friendly pathways, Australia & New Zealand offer excellent programs in Nursing, IT, Hospitality, Sports Science, Aviation, Architecture, Marine Science, Health Sciences and Business.

Study in Europe (Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, Sweden, Finland, Spain)

Europe offers affordable or free education. Germany excels in Engineering, Robotics, Automotive and IT. France leads in Fashion, Luxury Management, Culinary Arts and Hospitality. Italy is strong in Architecture, Design, Fine Arts and Music. Nordic countries specialise in Renewable Energy, Psychology and Game Design.

Study in the Middle East (UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia)

With rapidly growing education hubs, Middle Eastern countries offer programs in Aviation, Business, Event Management, Hotel Management, Sports Management, Cosmetology, Hair Styling, IT, Digital Marketing, Game Design, Nursing and Healthcare.
