Who We Are

Zhev Education is a leading study abroad consultancy that helps students across India achieve their global education goals. We provide transparent, ethical and personalised guidance for admissions to top universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia, Europe and the Middle East.

With years of experience and a commitment to student-first counselling, we guide you from course selection to visa approval and everything in between.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the study abroad journey for every student. We believe every student deserves accurate information, personalised guidance and a fair chance at international education opportunities.
