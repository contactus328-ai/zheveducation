Study Abroad Services

Zhev Education provides end-to-end support for international admissions—university shortlisting, SOP/LOR writing, application filing, scholarship search and visa preparation for students applying to USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia, Europe and the Middle East.

1. University Shortlisting

We curate personalised lists of Ambitious, Moderate and Safe universities based on your academics, test scores, experience and budget.

2. SOP & LOR Drafting

Professional, compelling Statements of Purpose and Letters of Recommendation tailored to your strengths and program fit.

3. Application Filing

We manage complete university applications—forms, documents, timelines and portal submissions for a smooth admissions journey.

4. Visa Guidance

Specialised support for visa documentation, financial planning, DS-160, biometrics and interview preparation.

5. Scholarship Assistance

We help identify university-based, government and external scholarships to reduce your overall cost of study.

6. Education Loan Support

Guidance for securing education loans from Indian banks, NBFCs and international lenders.
