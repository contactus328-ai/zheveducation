Who We Are

Zhev Education is a trusted study abroad consultancy helping students across India secure admissions in top universities worldwide. We offer end-to-end guidance for undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia, Europe and the Middle East.

Our expertise covers program selection, admissions strategy, SOP and LOR drafting, scholarship support, visa filing assistance and pre-departure planning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with transparent, ethical and personalised study abroad counselling. We help students identify the right course and country based on academic background, budget and long-term career goals.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert guidance for admission into top global universities
  • Professional SOP & LOR writing support
  • Visa documentation & mock interviews
  • Scholarship & education loan assistance
  • Complete support from application to arrival

Student Success

Students mentored by Zhev Education have secured admissions into leading universities across the USA, UK, Canada, Europe and Australia. Many have also received scholarships, assistantships and strong post-study work opportunities.
