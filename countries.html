Study in USA

The USA is a top destination for MS in Computer Science, Data Science and STEM programs with leading universities like MIT, Stanford, CMU and many public research institutions. Our services for Study in USA include university shortlisting, GRE/TOEFL/IELTS strategy, SOP & LOR preparation, scholarship searches, and F1 visa preparation. We advise on STEM OPT opportunities and long-term career planning.

Study in Canada

Canada offers strong PGWP pathways, affordable fees and growing tech hubs. We help students apply to top Canadian universities and colleges, prepare for study permits, and optimise applications for scholarship and PR pathways. Data Science and MS in CS are highly popular among Indian students.

Study in UK & Ireland

The UK has one-year Masters options and diverse scholarship opportunities. Ireland is a growing hub for tech talent with strong industry links. We provide targeted lists for one-year MSc programs, guidance on the Graduate Route (UK) and PSW options (Ireland), and interview/visa coaching.

Study in Australia & New Zealand

Australia and New Zealand provide quality programs with clear post-study work options. We guide students through intake choices, visa rules, recognition of credentials, and on-campus support.

How We Help You Choose

We evaluate your academic profile, budget, career goals and scholarship eligibility. For technical programs like MS in CS or Data Science, we recommend universities with strong research labs and internship pipelines. For management programs, our counsellors focus on placements and ROI.
