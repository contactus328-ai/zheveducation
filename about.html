Who We Are

Zhev Education is a leading study abroad consultancy helping students across India pursue higher education in top universities worldwide. We specialise in admissions for the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia and Europe with complete support for applications, SOPs, LORs, scholarships and visas.

Our experts guide students for programs including MS in CS, MBA, Data Science, Engineering, Hospitality, Architecture, Fashion, Design, MBBS, Nursing and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify global education for Indian students by offering ethical, transparent and result-driven counselling. We ensure every student receives personalised guidance based on academic background, budget and long-term career aspirations.
