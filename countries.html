Study in USA

The USA remains the top choice for STEM programs such as MS in Computer Science, Data Science, Cybersecurity, MBA, Engineering, Healthcare and even creative fields like Film, Music, Game Design, Fashion and Sports Management. With globally ranked universities, strong internship culture and OPT work visas, it provides excellent employment pathways.

Study in Canada

Canada offers career-focused education, PGWP work permits, PR pathways and a large international student community. Popular programs include MS in CS, Data Analytics, Nursing, Hospitality, Culinary Arts, Architecture, Sports Science and Business. Many universities offer affordable tuition and co-op options.

Study in UK & Ireland

The UK is known for 1-year Master's programs in management, engineering, fashion, design, sports, architecture and hospitality. Ireland is a booming tech hub with strong demand for CS, AI/ML, Data, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Pharmaceuticals and Healthcare programs. Both countries offer post-study work visas.

Study in Australia & New Zealand

Popular for programs in Nursing, Hospitality, Architecture, Construction, Marine Science, Sports Science, Fitness Training, MBBS pathways, Aviation, Business and Information Technology. Universities offer strong PR-friendly pathways and hands-on training.

Study in Europe (Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, Spain, Sweden, Finland)

Europe is a top destination for high-quality, low-cost education. Germany offers low or zero tuition fees, especially for Engineering, Robotics, Automotive, AI, Architecture and IT. France is strong for Fashion, Luxury Management, Culinary Arts and Hospitality. Italy is known for Design, Architecture, Fine Arts and Music. The Netherlands, Sweden and Finland offer globally ranked programs in Gaming, Psychology, Sports, Life Sciences, Renewable Energy and Engineering.

Study in the Middle East (UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia)

The Middle East is emerging as a premium, affordable education destination. Popular programs include Hotel Management, Aviation, Business, Sports Management, Medical Allied Courses, Fashion Styling, Hair & Beauty Therapy, Event Management, Digital Marketing and Game Design. UAE is especially known for hospitality, tourism, aviation and IT programs with strong internship opportunities.
