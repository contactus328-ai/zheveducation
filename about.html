Study in USA

The USA offers world-class education with unmatched opportunities in STEM (MS in CS, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, Data Science), Business (MBA), Engineering, Healthcare, Film, Music, Sports, Game Design, Fashion, and more. With OPT and CPT pathways, students gain strong job opportunities post-studies.

Study in Canada

Canada is ideal for PGWP pathways, high-quality education and permanent residency routes. Popular programs include MS in CS, Data Analytics, Nursing, Hospitality, Culinary Arts, MBBS pathways, Architecture, and Business. Co-op-based courses give strong industry exposure.

Study in UK & Ireland

UK offers a 1-year Master’s degree in fields such as Management, Engineering, Fashion, Sports Science, Hospitality, Architecture and Design. Ireland is a booming tech hub — excellent for CS, Data, Cloud, DevOps, Pharmaceuticals and Engineering with a 2-year post-study work visa.

Study in Australia & New Zealand

Known for PR-friendly pathways and great salaries in Nursing, Healthcare, IT, Architecture, Sports, Fitness Training, Aviation, Marine, Hotel Management, and Business. Their universities are globally ranked and offer hands-on, practical education.

Study in Europe (Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, Sweden, Finland, Spain)

Europe offers affordable or tuition-free education. Germany excels in Engineering, Robotics, Automotive, IT and Architecture. France is top-tier for Fashion, Luxury, Culinary Arts & Hospitality. Italy leads in Design, Architecture, Fine Arts and Music. Nordic countries excel in Gaming, Psychology, Renewable Energy and Engineering.

Study in Middle East (UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia)

The Middle East is rapidly becoming a global education hub. Popular programs include Hotel Management, Aviation, Business, Digital Marketing, Sports Management, Fashion Styling, Hair & Beauty Therapy, Game Design, Event Management and Medical Allied courses.
