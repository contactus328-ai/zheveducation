Our Mission & Experience

Zhev Education was founded to simplify the complex process of studying abroad for Indian students. Our mission is to provide transparent, practical guidance for admissions to top ranked programs — including MS in Computer Science, MBA programs, Data Science masters, and healthcare degrees. Our consultants have industry experience in university admissions, test preparation strategies and visa procedures. We curate personalised university shortlists for the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand with a focus on outcomes — scholarship chances, employability, and post-study work pathways.

Our Approach

We use a step-by-step process: eligibility assessment, program matching, application calendar, document preparation (SOP/LOR/resume), scholarship research, and visa interview coaching. For program specific help like MS in CS or Data Science, we provide targeted SOP revisions and technical resume alignment that highlights projects, internships and publications.

Team & Credentials

Our team includes former admission officers, test prep specialists and visa consultants. We maintain direct relationships with partner universities for timely updates on cutoffs, scholarships and intake options. We also support education loan assistance and pre-departure briefings so students travel prepared and confident.
