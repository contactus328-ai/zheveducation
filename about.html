About Zhev Education

Zhev Education is a Mumbai-based study abroad consultancy helping students secure admissions to reputable universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland and Australia. We provide personalised counseling, data-driven shortlists and end-to-end application support.

Our approach

We assess profile-fit (academics, tests, experience), create tiered shortlists (ambitious, target, safe), craft program-specific SOPs, coordinate LORs, and support visa readiness. Our process is transparent and results-oriented.

Local presence & support

With local Mumbai support, we offer in-person consultations, document verification and mock visa interviews. We also provide scholarship research and education loan guidance.
