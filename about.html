Our Services

Zhev Education provides complete end-to-end support for students planning to study abroad. Whether you’re applying to the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia, Europe or UAE, our expert counsellors help you build a strong, competitive application with personalised guidance at every step.

What We Offer

1. University Shortlisting

We analyse your academic background, test scores, projects, internships and budget to recommend target, ambitious and safe universities. We specialise in MS in CS, MBA, Data Science, AI/ML, Engineering, Healthcare, Hospitality, Architecture, Fashion, MBBS and more.

2. SOP & LOR Drafting

Our expert writers create impactful SOPs and LORs tailored to the program and university. Each document undergoes multiple reviews to ensure clarity, originality and alignment with admissions expectations.

3. Application Filing

We assist with all application portals, document uploads, essay checks, resume formatting, transcript evaluation (WES) and deadline tracking.

4. Scholarship Guidance

We help students identify university-funded and external scholarships, tuition waivers, research or teaching assistantships and financial planning for study abroad.

5. Visa Assistance

Our visa experts support with documentation, DS-160 filing, GTE/SOP for visas, mock interviews, sponsorship letters and financial templates for USA, UK, Canada, Australia and Ireland.

6. Pre-Departure & Accommodation

We help students with accommodation options, forex, travel planning, insurance, packing lists and university registrations after arrival.

Service Packages

  • Apply Smart: Shortlisting + SOP Review + Application Check
  • Apply Complete: Full Filing + LOR + Scholarships
  • Visa Secure: Visa Documents + Mock Interviews
  • All-Inclusive: Full admissions + visa + pre-departure support
