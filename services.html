Our Services

Zhev Education provides complete study abroad support for students applying to universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, Europe and the Middle East. From shortlisting universities to visa approval, our team ensures a smooth and stress-free admissions journey.

1. University Shortlisting

We curate a personalised list of Ambitious, Moderate and Safe universities based on your academics, experience, test scores, country preferences and budget.

2. SOP & LOR Drafting

Our expert writing team creates strong, personalised Statements of Purpose and Letters of Recommendation that highlight your strengths, achievements and program fit.

3. Application Filing

We assist with university portals, UCAS, CommonApp, OUAC, UniAssist and more. This includes essay reviews, resume creation, document checks, uploads and deadlines.

4. Scholarship Guidance

We help students identify and apply for university scholarships, government grants, tuition waivers and assistantship opportunities.

5. Visa Assistance

Our visa team provides document checklists, form assistance and mock interviews for USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia and Dubai student visas.

6. Education Loan Assistance

We guide you through education loan documentation, bank options, collateral requirements and interest rates.

7. Pre-Departure Guidance

Includes support for accommodation, forex, travel packing, airport guidance, insurance and arrival planning.
