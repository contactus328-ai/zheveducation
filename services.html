Complete Study Abroad Support

At Zhev Education, we provide end-to-end support for students aspiring to study abroad. Our services are designed to simplify the application journey—from choosing the right universities to preparing strong applications and completing visa documentation. We specialise in helping students pursue programs such as MS in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, MBA, Healthcare, Architecture, Design and many more in USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand.

Our counsellors analyse your academic profile, goals and financial plan to create customised pathways that increase your chances of admission and scholarships. Whether you're applying for MS in CS, MBA, Engineering, Nursing or any specialized program, our strategic guidance ensures your application stands out in competitive admissions cycles.

Our Core Services

1. University Shortlisting

We evaluate your profile, preferences, and career aspirations to suggest universities that match your goals. For technical courses such as Computer Science, Data Science, AI/ML, Cybersecurity and Engineering, we recommend programs with strong labs, research output, internship opportunities and industry tie-ups. For MBA and Management programs, we focus on ROI, placements and global rankings.

2. SOP & LOR Drafting

Our expert writers prepare SOPs that highlight your strengths, academic achievements, technical projects, internships and motivations. We craft LORs that align with university expectations and enhance the credibility of your application. Every document undergoes multiple revisions to ensure quality, clarity and uniqueness.

3. Application Filing & Document Support

We take care of application form filling, essay reviews, resume structuring, document uploads, transcript formatting, deadline tracking and portal submissions. Students receive full guidance on courier documents, WES evaluations and financial documentation.

4. Scholarship & Loan Assistance

We help students find university-funded scholarships, government scholarships, partial tuition waivers, assistantships and external funding options. For education loans, we support students in understanding eligibility criteria and connecting with lenders offering collateral and non-collateral loans.

5. Visa Filing & Interview Preparation

Our visa support includes compiling forms, checking financial proofs, preparing travel history documentation and conducting detailed mock interviews. We coach students for USA F1 visa interviews, UK Student Visa SOPs, Canada SDS documentation and Australia GTE requirements.

6. Pre-Departure & Post-Arrival Support

We guide students on accommodation, health insurance, forex, SIM cards, travel plans, packing lists and local transportation. Post-arrival support includes orientation on survival skills, tax ID applications, bank accounts and university onboarding.
