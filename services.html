Our Services

Zhev Education offers end-to-end study abroad support for students applying to the USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, Europe and the Middle East. We help you build a strong profile, shortlist the right universities and file applications with accuracy and clarity.

What We Offer

1. University Shortlisting

Based on your academics, test scores, budget and experience, we create a personalised list of Ambitious, Moderate and Safe universities. We specialise in MS in CS, AI/ML, MBA, Healthcare, Data Science, Engineering, Fashion, Architecture, Hospitality, MBBS and more.

2. SOP & LOR Drafting

Our expert writers help you craft impactful Statements of Purpose and Letters of Recommendation, ensuring they highlight your strengths, achievements and program alignment.

3. Application Filing

We guide you through CommonApp, UCAS, OUAC, UniAssist, university portals and other platforms. Includes essay reviews, resume creation, document uploads and deadline tracking.

4. Scholarship Guidance

We help you apply for university-funded, government and private scholarships, tuition waivers, RA/TA positions and grants to reduce study costs.

5. Visa Assistance

From documentation to mock interviews, our visa experts help you file USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia and Dubai student visas with confidence.

6. Pre-Departure Assistance

We guide you on accommodation, forex, insurance, travel planning, packing, arrival support and university onboarding.

Service Packages

  • Apply Smart: Shortlisting + SOP review + Application check
  • Complete Apply: Filing + SOP + LOR + Documents + Guidance
  • Visa Secure: Visa filing + documents + mock interviews
  • All-Inclusive: Full admissions to pre-departure support
